Peru 2024

In 2024 I went on a two-week trip to Peru from June 11th to June 25th. Here are some of the photos that I took during my trip. The cities and towns I visited were Lima, Ica, Nasca, Cusco, Urubamba, and Ollantaytambo. Coincidentally, I arrived in Cusco in the lead up to the Festival of the Sun (June 24th).
